EOS 90D - DSLR Camera

The EOS 90D camera is engineered to deliver impressive results. Equipped with an enhanced 32.5 Megapixel CMOS sensor, powerful DIGIC 8 image processor and an ISO range of 100-25000, you’ll achieve sharp, detailed images, even in low-light settings

EOS 7D - DSLR Camera

The EOS 7D features a refined APS-C sized 20.2 Megapixel CMOS sensor with Dual DIGIC 6 Image Processor. It shoots 10 frames per second at ISOs ranging from 100–16000, has a 65 all cross-type AF system and features Canon's amazing Dual Pixel CMOS AF for brilliant Live-View

EOS 6D - DSLR Camera

It features a 20.2 Megapixel Full-Frame CMOS sensor, a wide ISO range of 100-25600, expandable to L: 50, H1: 51200, and H2: 102400, for incredible image quality even in low light, and a DIGIC 5+ Image Processor delivers enhanced noise reduction and exceptional processing speed
